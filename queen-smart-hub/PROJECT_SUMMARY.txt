🔧 MQTT CONFIGURATION
================================================================================

Broker:   wss://broker.hivemq.com:8884/mqtt
Protocol: MQTT over WebSocket Secure

Topics:
  queen/lampu/1/control   → Control
  queen/lampu/1/status    ← Status
  queen/lampu/2/control
  queen/lampu/2/status
  queen/lampu/3/control
  queen/lampu/3/status

Message Format:
  {"state":"ON"}
  {"state":"OFF"}
